About Us

Our mission and vision

Our Mission

At Protect Me Albania, we are committed to improving the lives of stray and abandoned dogs throughout Albania. We provide shelter, medical care, and love to dogs in need, while working towards finding them forever homes.

What We Do

  • Rescue dogs from dangerous situations
  • Provide medical treatment and rehabilitation
  • Find loving forever homes through adoption
  • Educate the public about animal welfare
  • Advocate for improved animal protection laws
Read Our History

Our History

The journey of Protect Me Albania

2015

The Beginning

Protect Me Albania was founded by a group of passionate animal lovers who were concerned about the growing stray dog population in Tirana. Starting with just a small rented space, our founders began rescuing dogs in critical condition.

2017

Official NGO Status

After two years of grassroots operations, we officially registered as a non-profit organization, allowing us to expand our reach and establish partnerships with international animal welfare organizations.

2019

Opening Our Main Shelter

With the help of generous donors, we purchased land and built our main shelter facility, capable of housing up to 100 dogs at a time. We also launched our first major spay/neuter campaign.

2021

International Adoption Program

We established partnerships with shelters across Europe to facilitate international adoptions, giving many of our dogs the opportunity to find homes abroad.

2023

Educational Outreach

We launched our educational program, visiting schools and communities to teach about responsible pet ownership and compassion for animals.

Today

Continuing Our Mission

Today, Protect Me Albania continues to grow and expand its impact. With the support of our community and donors worldwide, we have rescued thousands of dogs and continue to work towards a day when no dog in Albania is without a loving home.

Your Support Makes a Difference

Every donation, no matter the size, helps us provide food, shelter, and medical care to dogs in need. Your generosity directly impacts the lives of these animals and gives them a chance at a better future.

What Your Donation Can Provide:

  • €10 can feed a dog for a week
  • €25 can provide vaccinations
  • €50 can sponsor basic medical treatment
  • €100 can help with spay/neuter surgeries
  • €200+ can help with emergency surgeries and special cases
